My Story...

I have loved and owned dogs all my life. In 2024, I lost my beloved Tito, a rescue, wh I loved for over 12 years. I retired in October 2024, alone with my husband. Since we are planning on travelling over the next few years, we have decided not to adopt another pet at the moment. However, we both miss having a dog to love, cuddle, and play with. What started out as watching a friend's pups while she was away, grew into a small business.

With over 10 years experience volunteering at local animal shelters, you can feel secure leaving your dog(s) with me. I only host one dog family at a time. All my love and attention will be for your dog(s) only. I do my best to accomodate your dog's schedule and needs, so they feel as cofortable as possible in my home while you are away. I have a large fenced in backyard where they are never left unattended. I will take them on walks and provide daily play time if your dog enjoys that!

Let me know if your dog has any special needs or habits, so I am able to accomodate theur individul personalities. Dogs are as unique as people and loved for their individuality.
