My Story...

I have loved and owned dogs all my life. In 2024, I lost my beloved Tito, a rescue, who I loved for over 12 years. I retired in October 2024, along with my husband. Since we are planning on travelling over the next few years, we have decided not to adopt another pet at the moment. However, we both miss having a dog to love, cuddle, and play with. What started out as watching a friend's pups while she was away, grew into a small business.

With over 10 years experience volunteering at local animal shelters, you can feel secure leaving your dog(s) with me. I only host one dog family at a time. All my love and attention will be for your dog(s) only. I do my best to accommodate your dog's schedule and needs, so they feel as comfortable as possible in my home while you are away. I have a large fenced in backyard where they are never left unattended. I will take them on walks and provide daily play time if your dog enjoys that!

Let me know if your dog has any special needs or habits, so I am able to accommodate their individual personalities. Dogs are as unique as people and loved for their individuality.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are your Holiday rates?

I currently do not charge Holiday rates.

Can we meet before booking your services?

A meet and greet is required prior to the first booking. I want to make sure your dog(s) like and feel comfortable with me.

What types of dog(s) do you watch?

All breeds are welcome and I love dogs of all types, however, I do not watch dog(s) over 45 lbs.

How far in advance should I book?

Because I only watch one family at a time, I get booked pretty far in advance, especially for school And holiday breaks. Please contact me as far in advance as possible. I will pencil you in until another family requests the same time. At this point, you will be asked to commit and leave a deposit to secure the booking.

How many dogs can you watch at once?

Since I have no pets and only host one family at a time, I can host up to 4 dogs.

What do I need to provide to you for overnight boarding?

You will provide enough food for your dog(s) entire stay. Please bring a harness/collar or leash for each dog. Any sweaters/jackets for cold weather, if needed. I do have dog beds, blankets, and a dog crate and food bowls, but you are welcome to bring their own if that makes them more comfortable.

What is your cancellation policy?

I am flexible with cancellations, up to 2 days prior to drop off date. Less than 48 hours will result in forfeit of deposit since I had to turn away other families during those booked dates.

Why does it cost more for a puppy?

Puppies require a lot more love and care, frequent potty breaks, and tend to have frequent accidents in the house.

Who else is in your household that my dog(s) would be staying with?

My husband Tommy, who is also retired, and myself are the only people in the home. He loves dogs also and they always love him back. No young children visit and we refrain from having visitors when dog(s) are visiting.
